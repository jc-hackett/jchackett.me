The main site is a practice site. It needs to stay professional and legible to strangers in under thirty seconds.

This is different. These are notes — short things that are finished enough to post but don't need to be essays. Memos. Fragments that deserve a URL.

I've been meaning to have a space like this for a while. Some things I want to say in public are too brief for an article and too specific for a social post. Notes seem like the right container.

No comment section. No newsletter. No particular schedule. Just things worth saying, briefly.
